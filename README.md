## 🌐 Overview

**I-do-Guide** is a web-based platform designed specifically for **Pakistan** to simplify complex government procedures and student-related processes through easy, step-by-step guidance.

It helps users understand how to apply for official documents such as **CNIC, passports, domicile certificates**, and access educational opportunities like **scholarships, admissions, and internships**—without confusion or reliance on middlemen.

---

## 🇵🇰 Pakistan-Focused Services

* 🪪 CNIC / B-Form application guidance
* 🛂 Passport application process
* 📜 Domicile & character certificates
* 🎓 University admissions (HEC recognized institutions)
* 💰 Scholarships (HEC, Ehsaas, provincial programs)
* 💼 Internship & job application guides
